2. Description of Service

CoworkX is a modular productivity workspace for macOS. The App provides local tools including clipboard management, secure credential storage, local database querying, calendar workflows, local AI connectivity, YouTube API integrations, analytics views, and RSS feed management.

The App is available in a Free tier and offers additional features through auto-renewable subscription plans (Pro, Power, and Business).

3. Subscriptions and Payments

3.1 Auto-Renewable Subscriptions

CoworkX offers the following auto-renewable subscription tiers:

  • Pro — $29/month: Unlocks Secure Vault, Local Database, and Notifications.
  • Power — $69/month: Unlocks Calendar Access, Local AI Gateway, YouTube API, Analytics, and RSS & Feeds.
  • Business — $149/month: Unlocks all modules, audit logging, and priority support.

3.2 Billing and Renewal

Payment will be charged to your Apple ID account at confirmation of purchase. Subscriptions automatically renew unless auto-renew is turned off at least 24 hours before the end of the current period. Your account will be charged for renewal within 24 hours prior to the end of the current period.

3.3 Managing Subscriptions

You can manage and cancel your subscriptions by going to your Apple ID account settings on your Mac or iOS device after purchase. Turn off auto-renewal to cancel your subscription.
